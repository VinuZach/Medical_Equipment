Welcome to Matha Institute of Paramedical Sciences and Nursing School.
Since our inception in 2016, Matha Institute, also known as TKN institution, functioned with the lofty vision of imparting progressive education suited to the changing nature of the society, that provides 100 percent job-oriented courses with excellent coaching and training, with a focus on women's empowerment.
VALUES
As individuals and together, Matha Institution is built on the foundations of moral and ethical standards that are in line with the aim of keeping the education, health and welfare of society at the forefront.
MISSION
Development of self-existence in women through the provision of student-centered, quality education defined by excellence, efficiency and higher values.
VISION
In everything we do, we strive to be the best, always striving for excellence committed to uplifting society and upholding noble principles.
OBJECTIVES
At Matha Institution, we strive to provide all people with the dignity of job, particularly in the fields of education and medicine, to all those who come to us with deep expectations.

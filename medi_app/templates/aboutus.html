About Us

History

To keep up with the rapidly changing modern world, specialty-based quality education is an absolute To keep up with the rapidly changing modern world, specialty-based quality education is an absolute necessity. With this objective in mind, the Matha institution was founded in 2016 in the heart of Marayoor. Established and managed by Dr. Subburam and Mrs. Devi Subburaman, our ambition is to provide a world-class platform for professional learning and training in the fields of paramedical sciences and nursing, where our students can seize the opportunity and the challenge to do what they are capable of and reach their full potential on all fronts, both curricular and extracurricular. In addition to being the finest paramedical and nursing institute in the country, our institute is distinguished by its skilled teachers, efficient administrative staff and quality infrastructure, that gives students an opportunity to shape the future and reimagine the world.

Aim and objectives

At Matha Institution, we are dedicated to encouraging, promoting, and propagating best-in-class technical and specialized education. Our aims and objectives include:



  • To bring a better understanding of the people's problems

  • To stay in close contact with people

  • To provide students with comprehensive technology-integrated education for a better career

  • To arrange meetings, seminars, etc
VALUES
As individuals and together, Matha Institution is built on the foundations of moral and ethical standards that are in line with the aim of keeping the education, health and welfare of society at the forefront.
MISSION
Development of self-existence in women through the provision of student-centered, quality education defined by excellence, efficiency and higher values.
VISION
In everything we do, we strive to be the best, always striving for excellence committed to uplifting society and upholding noble principles.
OBJECTIVES
